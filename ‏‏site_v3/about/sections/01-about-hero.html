About Our Platform

Redefining Resilience:
From Trauma to Opportunity

The Resilience & Health Innovation Hub in Sderot and the Western Negev was born from a unique, challenging reality. For decades, this region has served as the frontline of community and security crisis. Today, we are transforming that experience into a global epicentre of technological innovation.


In a world increasingly shaped by climate challenges, societal stress, and medical disruptions, resilience is no longer just a word — it is an urgent global market. We connect local insights with world-class expertise to build technologies that save lives and stabilize societies.

We believe that those closest to the challenge are the most qualified to solve it. By bridging regional data with international networks, we create high-impact, scalable businesses.
