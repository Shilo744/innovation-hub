Vision & Mission

Transforming Sderot into a global catalyst for health & resilience.

"Where the frontline of crisis becomes the frontier of breakthrough technologies."

The Western Negev region represents a unique living laboratory. The challenges encountered here demand rapid, robust solutions. By building a unified resilience tech platform, we convert stress into scalable, investable assets that address clinical and community needs across the globe.

Our Vision

To build a leading global ecosystem where academic researchers, healthcare systems, municipal leaders, and entrepreneurs co-create resilience innovations that transform society.

Our Mission

To validate, accelerate, and deploy technologies in trauma management, mental health, and community continuity, ensuring robust economic growth for the Western Negev and tangible help worldwide.

Sderot: A Global Resilient Sandbox

Sderot’s local community infrastructure provides a rare real-world laboratory. Founders can pilot emotional-support systems, trauma diagnostics, and critical services continuity tools in real-time, real-world conditions. This sandbox generates rapid feedback, de-risking technology early in its lifecycle.

Unparalleled Economic Potential

The global resilience and mental health market is projected to reach hundreds of billions of dollars. Participating in our sandbox offers direct access to comprehensive datasets, academic resources from Sapir College, clinical guidance from Sheba Medical Center, and substantial state incentives designed to drive innovation.
