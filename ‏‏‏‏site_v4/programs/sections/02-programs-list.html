Flagship · Accelerator 01

Resilience Accelerator

Our flagship equity-free program for early-stage startups building the next generation of resilience technologies, from mental health platforms and crisis-response infrastructure to clinical recovery tools. Six months of deep operator support, clinical pilot pathways, investor introductions, and a global community of founders.

Flagship · Community 02

Sheba to Sderot

A unique flagship program run through close collaboration between Sheba Medical Center, the Sderot Municipality, and the Innovation Hub. It brings leading clinicians, researchers, and entrepreneurs to the front line of community health resilience, advancing local pilots with global impact potential. Starting in Sderot, reaching the world.

Deployment 03

Immediate Implementation

A rapid-deployment track that takes vetted, ready-to-use clinical and operational solutions and routes them straight to the frontlines of community care within weeks. Designed for times when speed matters most and crisis response cannot wait for traditional development timelines.

Umbrella · 4 sub-programs 04

MEITAL

Our applied-research and co-development umbrella, bringing together four complementary tracks for long-term resilience innovation across clinical research, technical validation, community initiatives, and public policy formulation.

MEITAL · Clinical Translational research from bedside trauma diagnostics to scalable hospital-grade recovery protocols.
MEITAL · Technology Applied technology validating and benchmarking sensors, biosignal diagnostics, and AI remote-monitoring tools.
MEITAL · Community Empirical field research and co-designed technologies that strengthen localized, municipal municipal networks.
MEITAL · Policy Translating digital health evidence into systemic public policy recommendations and funding models.
