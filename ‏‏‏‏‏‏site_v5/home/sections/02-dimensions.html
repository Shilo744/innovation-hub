Framework

Three dimensions of sustainable resilience.

Resilience Research ARM

Provides infrastructure for sustainable impact, including advanced lab equipment, an incubator, and a research center. It funds clinical studies and pilot programs to accelerate proof of concept, using blended funding models that support local entrepreneurship and long-term regional impact.

Investment Arm

Offers strategic value for investors through early access to promising startups validated in Israel’s unique resilience "sandbox." It enables piloting based on extensive regional data, with a de-risked model leveraging public and philanthropic funding. The goal: dual returns of broad social impact and a stronger Israeli innovation economy.

Knowledge & Training ARM

Builds capacity through professional courses and training for entrepreneurs, practitioners, and policymakers. It promotes collaboration with academic and research institutions and enables the sharing of insights, research, and pilot outcomes to support dissemination and scaling.
