Redefining Resilience:
From Trauma to Opportunity

The Resilience & Health Innovation Hub in Sderot and the Western Negev was born from a challenging reality. For decades this region has lived on the frontline of community and security crisis — and the people here carry more practical knowledge about resilience than almost anywhere in the world.

We believe those closest to the challenge are the most qualified to solve it. The hub brings clinicians, researchers and founders to work alongside the community, turning that hard-earned experience into technologies that save lives and strengthen societies.
